## Story (GoFundMe "full story")

### What We're Doing

On June 12, 2026, a rotating team of singers will take the stage in the heart of Times Square and sing non-stop for **38 days and 912 hours** — attempting to break the Guinness World Record for the longest karaoke marathon ever recorded.

This isn't just a record attempt. It's a statement: **Gen-Z mental health matters, and we're not stopping until everyone knows it.**

---

### Why It Matters

Anxiety. Depression. Isolation. Gen-Z is facing a mental health crisis unlike any generation before — and the silence around it is deafening. We believe music can break that silence.

**Every dollar raised beyond production costs goes directly to Gen-Z mental health organizations.** We're not just setting a record. We're funding real support for real people.

---

### The Scale of This

To pull off 912 continuous hours of live karaoke in Times Square, we need:

- 🎤 **Professional PA system** — microphones, speakers, and monitors for 38 days of continuous use
- 🏗️ **Weather-resistant outdoor stage** — built to survive NYC summer weather, 24/7
- 📋 **NYC permits & GWR adjudication** — event permits, Guinness fees, liability insurance for the full run
- 📹 **Multi-camera recording setup** — Guinness requires continuous documented proof
- 🌙 **Overnight infrastructure** — lighting, safety, and logistics for 24/7 operation
- 👥 **Volunteer coordination** — 400+ singers across 38 days need scheduling, onboarding, and support

---

### Where We Are Now

- **240+ singers** already registered and in the pipeline
- **All registration and management software** built and ready (SunLog)
- **Night Owl Club** formed — singers committed to the overnight 10pm–7am shifts
- **Guinness compliance** framework in place
- **Times Square venue** in final negotiations

We've done the hard work. Now we need the resources to see it through.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I sing in the record?**
Yes! Registration is open at [kikistop.github.io](https://kikistop.github.io/sing-until-nobodys-gone/signup.html). No experience required.

**Where exactly in Times Square?**
Venue details will be announced in May 2026. Follow us for updates.

**What happens if you don't hit the goal?**
All donations are used for production costs regardless. Any shortfall comes from team resources — we are committed to this happening.

**Is this a Guinness-certified attempt?**
Yes. We are registering the attempt with Guinness World Records and will have adjudicators on-site.
