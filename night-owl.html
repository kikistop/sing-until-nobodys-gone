Why Overnight Matters

The Most Critical Shift

A Guinness World Record for continuous karaoke means exactly that: continuous. Not mostly continuous. Not almost continuous.

Every hour of every night — from midnight to 6am — someone has to be on that stage singing. No exceptions.

Daytime shifts are easy to fill. Prime Time (2pm–10pm) practically sells itself — it's Times Square, it's sunny, there's a crowd. But the overnight shift is a different story.

The overnight shift (10pm–7am) is the hardest to fill and the most critical to the record. Without Night Owls, the record attempt doesn't just slow down. It ends.

The math:

38 nights × 9 hours per night = 342 overnight hours to fill. That's 152+ individual 2-hour slots that need real people. We need you.

Night Owl Club Benefits

Exclusive Perks for Night Owls

Because the people keeping this record alive overnight deserve more than a pat on the back.

🎵

Priority Song Selection

Night Owls choose their songs before everyone else in the shift. You want to open with your all-time favorite? Done. No waiting, no compromises.

Submit your song list during registration and it will be pre-loaded when you arrive.

👕

Exclusive Night Owl Merch

Complete 2 or more overnight shifts and receive the limited-edition Night Owl Club tee and sticker pack. Only Night Owls ever have this. Not available in any store.

Merch mailed after the event. Estimated value: $40.

📱

Social Media Spotlight

Every overnight singer gets featured on the official SUN social accounts. We tell your story, share your performance, and tag you in. 38 nights of overnight content.

Mention your handles during registration and we'll make sure you get credit.

🏆

GWR Co-Breaker Credit

Your name is permanently part of the Guinness World Record documentation. Every night you sing, the record holds because of you. Your name goes on the certificate.

All participants are credited, but Night Owls get special acknowledgment in the GWR submission.
