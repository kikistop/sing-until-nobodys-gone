About the Campaign

Why Crowdfunding?

A 38-day world record attempt in Times Square costs serious money — sound equipment, permits, livestreaming, volunteer support, and legal compliance add up fast. Sponsors help, but we're building this with people, not just corporations.

Crowdfunding means backers like you are co-owners of this moment. Your name goes in the record. Your contribution is what makes it happen. Every dollar beyond production costs goes to Gen-Z mental health organizations.

We chose this path because if anyone should fund a world record about singing together, it should be the people who sing together.
