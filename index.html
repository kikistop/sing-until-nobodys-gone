The Mission

Music as a lifeline

Sing Until Nobody's Gone (SUN) is more than a world record — it's a statement. For 38 consecutive days, singers from across New York and beyond will take the stage in Times Square, keeping the music alive around the clock.

"Every voice matters. Every shift counts. Every song is a reminder that you are not alone."

The event raises awareness and funds for Gen-Z mental health. In a city that never sleeps, we're showing that community never has to either. We need 400+ volunteer singers — all backgrounds, all genres, all experience levels welcome.

The Night Owl Club

The Record Doesn't Sleep.
Neither Do We.

Why the overnight shift is the most important — and how to join the club that makes it possible.

Why Overnight Matters

A Guinness World Record for continuous karaoke means exactly that: continuous. Not mostly continuous. Not almost continuous.

Every hour of every night — from midnight to 6am — someone has to be on that stage singing. No exceptions.

The overnight shift (10pm–6am) is the hardest to fill and the most critical to the record. Without Night Owls, the record attempt doesn't just slow down. It ends.

That's why we created the Night Owl Club.

Who Are Night Owls?
  • Bar and restaurant workers — Off at midnight, karaoke-comfortable, energy to spare.
  • Healthcare night-shifters — You already have the schedule.
  • Students on summer break — No 8am class. No finals.
  • Content creators — Times Square at 3am is a different world.
  • K-pop and anime fans — Late-night events are your natural habitat.
  • Night owls by nature — Some people just don't sleep.

How It Works

Your 2 Hours Change Everything

Participating is simple. Show up, grab the mic, sing your heart out.

📝

1. Sign Up

Fill out the registration form with your preferred shift times and song choices.

📅

2. Get Confirmed

We'll email your confirmed shift time, check-in instructions, and a calendar invite.

📍

3. Show Up

Arrive 15 minutes early. Check in via QR code. The incoming volunteer coordinator will brief you.

🎤

4. Make History

Step up to the mic and sing. For 2 hours, you are the world record.
