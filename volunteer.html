Volunteer Sign-Up

Be Part of History

We need 100+ official witnesses, shift coordinators, and event support volunteers to make 38 days of non-stop karaoke happen. Join us June 12 – July 19, 2026.

🔍

Official Witness

Required by Guinness World Records. Observe and log performances, sign official declarations. Must be independent (not a singer or staff). 8-hour shifts.

📋

Shift Coordinator

Manage singer handoffs, ensure no singing gaps, coordinate with the event team. 4-6 coordinators needed across all 228 shifts.

🤝

Event Support

Welcome singers, handle check-in/check-out, support the audience, keep Times Square flowing smoothly throughout the 912 hours.
